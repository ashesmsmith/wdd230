Discover Rexburg, Idaho

Rexburg is a beautiful community located in South East Idaho.

Demographic Data

In 2021, Rexburg, ID had a population of 33.7k people with a median age of 22.8 and a median household income of $44,034. Between 2020 and 2021 the population of Rexburg, ID grew from 29,064 to 33,684, a 15.9% increase and its median household income grew from $33,278 to $44,034, a 32.3% increase.

97% of the residents in Rexburg, ID are U.S. citizens.

In 2021, the median property value in Rexburg, ID was $264,000, and the homeownership rate was 35.6%.

Data provided by: Data USA
